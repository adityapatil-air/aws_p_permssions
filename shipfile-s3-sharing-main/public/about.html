About ShipFile

We're building the future of secure file sharing by making AWS S3 accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise.

Our Mission

At ShipFile, we believe that powerful file sharing shouldn't require a computer science degree. Our mission is to democratize enterprise-grade file sharing by providing an intuitive interface for AWS S3, the world's most reliable cloud storage service.

We're committed to helping businesses of all sizes leverage the power of AWS S3 for secure, scalable file sharing without the complexity of traditional cloud storage management.

Our Values

Security First

We prioritize security in everything we do. Your data stays in your AWS account, and we never store or access your files.

User-Centric

We design with our users in mind, creating intuitive experiences that make complex cloud operations simple and accessible.

Innovation

We continuously innovate to bring you the latest features and improvements, staying ahead of the evolving needs of modern teams.

Our Story

ShipFile was born out of frustration with existing file sharing solutions. While AWS S3 offers unparalleled reliability and security, its complexity often puts it out of reach for many businesses and teams.

Our founders, experienced in both cloud infrastructure and user experience design, recognized the gap between powerful cloud storage capabilities and user-friendly interfaces. They set out to build a bridge that would make enterprise-grade file sharing accessible to everyone.

Today, ShipFile serves teams around the world, from small startups to large enterprises, helping them leverage the power of AWS S3 for secure, scalable file sharing and collaboration.
