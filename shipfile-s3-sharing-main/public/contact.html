Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with ShipFile?

Simply connect your AWS S3 bucket using your credentials. Our setup guide will walk you through the entire process.

Is my data secure?

Yes! Your files stay in your AWS S3 bucket. We never store or access your data - we only provide the interface.

Do I need technical knowledge to use ShipFile?

Not at all! Our intuitive interface makes it easy for anyone to use, regardless of technical background.

What AWS permissions do I need?

You'll need S3 access permissions. Our documentation provides detailed setup instructions for AWS IAM configuration.
